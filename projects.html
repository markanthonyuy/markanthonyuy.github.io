Personal

During my pastime I like to think of how can I solve problems. Just simple problems that I'm having. I remember a colleague of mine first made a Manga reader. I was in awe and got really inspired from that day. Since I love reading manga, I told myself that what is the problem that I mostly encounter. The answers are LOADING SPEED, POOR UI and Ads everywhere! If you're reading manga definitely you are aware of these problems, besides manga images are extremely huge the sites are always bombarded with ads. Quite annoying. I don't blame them though if that's their monetization strategy. Anyway, I just took action on the matter and build my own Manga Reader. And Libremanga was born. (FYI this is not maintained.) My first web app. Then the rest is history :)
